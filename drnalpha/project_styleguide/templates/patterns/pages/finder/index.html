Business regulation checker

Check the regulations that apply to your business all in one place. You can see:

Before you start

All you need to do is supply some basic information about your business. You’ll need to know:

  • where your business operates
  • how many employees you have
  • what basis you employ people on, e.g. full-time or part-time
  • the activities your business carries out.
